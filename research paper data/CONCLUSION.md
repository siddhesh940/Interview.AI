# V. CONCLUSION

Interview.ai successfully addresses the gap in placement preparation by providing AI-driven practice sessions that simulate real interview scenarios. Through our testing, users showed noticeable improvement after just a few practice sessions, with features like voice-based interaction, instant feedback, Resume Builder, and Time Machine helping them track growth and identify weak areas. The combination of company-specific preparation and gamified modules makes the learning process both practical and engaging, proving that AI can meaningfully enhance interview preparation for students. The system's ability to provide personalized feedback and track progress over time gives students the confidence they need to perform better in actual interviews. This research demonstrates that technology can bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, making quality interview training accessible to all students regardless of their background or resources.
