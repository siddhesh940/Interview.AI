# Introduction

Interview.ai is an AI-powered career preparation platform designed to help job seekers enhance their interview readiness and overall professional skills. The system provides AI-driven mock interviews with real-time voice, facial expression, and response analysis to evaluate confidence, communication, and technical accuracy. It also includes an intelligent resume builder with industry-specific templates, company-focused preparation modules for organizations such as TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Cognizant, and Capgemini, and a structured placement drive management system. Additional features like Aptitude Arena, soft skills training, interview-based games, and the Time Machine module enable continuous skill improvement and progress tracking. By integrating artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and analytics, Interview.ai bridges the gap between traditional interview preparation and modern AI-based learning, offering a personalized, scalable, and effective career readiness solution.
