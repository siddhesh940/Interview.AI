## Week-wise Detailed Breakdown

### Week 1 — Review of Major Project-I & Planning (08/01/26)
- Reviewed all modules completed in Major Project-I (User Authentication, Voice Agent, Multimodal Analysis, Basic UI, Backend API)
- Identified remaining features for Phase-II implementation
- Created development roadmap and task distribution among team members
- Set up project milestones and weekly targets

### Week 2 — Sentiment Analysis & Suggestions (15/01/26)
- Developed Sentiment Analysis section with core metrics:
  - Overall Sentiment Score, Clarity Score, Confidence Score, Overall Impression Score
- Soft Skills Assessment: Teamwork, Adaptability, Empathy, Communication
- Personalized Suggestions with Strengths, Areas for Improvement & Recommendations
- Hesitation count, average response time, color-coded progress bars

### Week 3 — Facial Recognition Analysis (22/01/26)
- Implemented FacialRecognitionAnalysis component with:
  - Emotion Summary Chart (happy, neutral, nervous, angry, confused, surprised)
  - Confidence Index (0-100) based on eye contact, posture, facial stability
  - Engagement Score measuring attentiveness and expressiveness
  - Stress Indicator for tension/anxiety detection
- Created FacialAnalysisService and API endpoint

### Week 4 — Interactive Games Module (29/01/26)
- Developed 6 interactive interview skill-building games
- Added Games section to sidebar with Gamepad2 icon
- Implemented game logic, scoring system, and leaderboards
- Gamified learning approach for interview preparation

### Week 5 — Review-1 (05/02/26)
- Demonstrated Sentiment Analysis, Facial Recognition & Games modules to guide
- Received feedback on UI improvements and feature enhancements
- Discussed remaining feature priorities
- Made corrections based on review suggestions

### Week 6 — Aptitude Arena & TIPE (12/02/26)
- Built Aptitude Arena with 1000+ questions across aptitude, verbal & logical reasoning
- Developed Technical Interview Practice Engine (TIPE) covering 10 domains:
  - DSA, DBMS, OS, OOP, Computer Networks, C, C++, Java, Python, JavaScript
- 4 Practice Modes: Quick Practice, Timed Challenge, Topic Deep Dive, Mock Interview
- AI-powered evaluation and adaptive difficulty

### Week 7 — Resume Builder & Management (19/02/26)
- Developed professional Resume Builder with:
  - ATS-optimized templates, AI-powered content enhancement, PDF export
  - Multi-step builder: Personal Info → Education → Experience → Skills → Preview
- Resume Management System with auto-save, CRUD operations, database integration
- Row Level Security policies for user-specific storage

### Week 8 — Placement Drives System (26/02/26)
- Automated web scraping for 6 companies (Accenture, TCS, Capgemini, Wipro, Cognizant, Infosys)
- Smart Eligibility Matching engine (Batch, CGPA, Branch based)
- Real-time notification system for new eligible drives
- Interactive dashboard with Eligible Drives, All Drives, and Notifications tabs
- Backend scheduler running every 12 hours

### Week 9 — Time Machine (05/03/26)
- AI Future Self Predictor with resume upload and parsing (PDF, DOCX)
- Career prediction based on skills, experience and target role
- Generates: Skill progression, Salary predictions, Project recommendations, Achievement milestones
- Learning roadmap for 30/60/90 day goals
- Integrated pdf-parse, pdfjs-dist with OCR fallback

### Week 10 — HR Coach, Soft Skills & Dream Company (12/03/26)
- HR Interview Coach for HR round mastery with AI-powered practice
- Soft Skills Training module with communication, body language, and presentation tips
- Dream Company Station with company-specific preparation for 6 major companies
- Interview Resource Hub with 30+ PDF resources
